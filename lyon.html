Lyon

Lyon or Lyons (UK: /ˈliːɒ̃/,[4][5] US: /liˈoʊn/,[6][7][c] French: [ljɔ̃] (About this soundlisten); Arpitan: Liyon, pronounced [ʎjɔ̃]; Italian: Lione, pronounced [liˈone]) is the third-largest city and second-largest urban area of France. It is located at the confluence of the rivers Rhône and Saône, about 470 km (292 mi) south of Paris, 320 km (199 mi) north of Marseille and 56 km (35 mi) northeast of Saint-Étienne. Inhabitants of the city are called Lyonnais.
